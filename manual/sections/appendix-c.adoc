== APPENDIX C: APPROVED WIS 2.0 CENTRES

*1. GENERAL*

1.1 The WIS centres previously approved by Congress or the Executive Council, included in the list in Appendix B of the _Manual on the WMO Information System_ (WMO-No. 1060), Volume I, are candidate WIS 2.0 centres.

1.2 *WIS centres shall complete the migration from WIS/GTS to WIS 2.0 in order to be designated as WIS 2.0 centres and added to the list in the present Appendix.*
